Got Questions?

We Have Answers

01

Where is the company headquartered?

The company is headquartered in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, at Churchill Avenue, Eshetu Mamo Building – F8 Office 801.

02

What countries does the company import and export to?

The company imports and exports to countries all over the world. Our extensive network allows us to facilitate trade across multiple continents, connecting Ethiopian producers with international markets across Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and the Americas.

03

What products does the company import and export?

We import raw materials, machinery, tyres, steel bars, and consumer goods. For exports, we specialize in oilseeds (sesame, niger seed, soybean, black cumin), pulses (chickpeas, green mung beans, red kidney beans), and premium Ethiopian coffees (Yirgacheffe Grade 2, Lekempti Grade 4, Jimma Grade 5).

04

How can I request a quote?

You can request a quote by visiting our Request a Quote page and completing the form. Alternatively, call us or email info@azmerawbekele.com.

05

What certifications and memberships does the company hold?

We are proud members of the Ethiopian Commodity Exchange (ECX), FIATA, and other recognized international trade bodies. These memberships ensure we uphold the highest standards of trade practice and can access global trade networks effectively.

06

How long has the company been in business?

Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export was established in 2011 with a paid-up capital of Birr 20,000,000.00. The owner has over 20 years of experience in the import and export industry, bringing deep expertise to every transaction.

07

Does the company provide warehousing and logistics services?

Yes. We provide secure and reliable warehousing solutions to store your goods before, during, and after transit. We also offer distribution and full supply chain management services to optimize efficiency and minimize costs.

08

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary depending on the product and destination. Please contact our sales team or use the Request a Quote form to discuss your specific requirements and receive a personalized offer.
