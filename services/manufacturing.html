Your Partner in Manufacturing Excellence

Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export stands as a beacon of innovation and quality in Ethiopia's manufacturing landscape. Our state-of-the-art PP Woven bags manufacturing facility combines cutting-edge technology with skilled craftsmanship to produce high-quality bags for a variety of applications.

Our manufacturing operations create employment for over 300 individuals, contributing significantly to Ethiopia's economic development. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality while minimizing environmental impact through responsible production practices.

"Your Partner in Manufacturing Excellence"

Our Production Process

PP Extruding & Circular Weaving

Raw polypropylene is extruded into tapes and then woven into fabric using advanced circular looms.

Flexo & Gravure Printing

High-quality multi-color printing using both flexographic and gravure printing technologies for branding.

Lamination & Finishing

Lamination for moisture protection, followed by cutting, LDPE/HDPE inserting, gusseting, and sewing.

Quality Commitment

Material Selection

Only the highest quality raw materials are used, ensuring durability and consistency in every bag produced.

State-of-the-Art Machinery

Modern automated machinery from leading manufacturers ensures precision and efficiency throughout production.

Quality Control

Rigorous quality control checks at every production stage ensure that every bag meets international standards.

Social Impact: Our manufacturing facility provides employment for over 300 individuals, many of whom come from underserved communities. We invest in training and skills development, creating a workforce that contributes to Ethiopia's broader industrial growth.
