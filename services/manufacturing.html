Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export stands as a beacon of innovation and quality in Ethiopia's manufacturing landscape. Our state-of-the-art PP Woven bags manufacturing facility exemplifies our commitment to producing high-quality products while contributing to the nation's economic growth.

Our comprehensive production process encompasses:

  • PP extruding: We transform raw polypropylene into plastic strands, the foundation of our PP Woven bags.

  • Circular weaving: Our advanced weaving machines interweave the PP strands to create durable and resilient fabric.

  • Flexo printing: We utilize flexographic printing technology to apply vibrant and precise designs onto the PP Woven bags.

  • Gravure printing: For intricate and detailed designs, our gravure printing method delivers exceptional results.

  • Lamination: We enhance the strength and durability of our PP Woven bags by applying a protective laminate layer.

  • Bags cutting: Our precision cutting machines transform the PP Woven fabric into bags of various sizes and specifications.

  • LDPE/HDPE inserting: For added strength and protection, we incorporate LDPE or HDPE liners into our PP Woven bags as needed.

  • Gusseting: We create expandable gussets to accommodate varying product volumes and enhance bag functionality.

  • Bags sewing: Our experienced seamstresses ensure the bags are securely stitched for optimal performance.

Our quality is not just a goal; it's our unwavering commitment. We adhere to stringent quality control measures throughout our production process, ensuring that every PP Woven bag meets the highest standards.
