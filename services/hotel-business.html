A New Standard of Hospitality in Jiga Town

Currently under development, our hotel business is poised to become a premier hospitality establishment in Jiga town, Amhara region of Ethiopia. This ambitious project reflects our commitment to contributing to Ethiopia's tourism and hospitality sector, creating new economic opportunities in the region.

Our hotel development plan incorporates modern design standards, world-class amenities, and a commitment to showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the Amhara region. We are working with experienced hospitality consultants and architects to ensure our hotel meets international standards while celebrating local culture.
