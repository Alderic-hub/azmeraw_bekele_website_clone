Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export is a leading provider of transportation services, specializing in the efficient and timely delivery of goods across Ethiopia. Our comprehensive suite of transportation solutions caters to a diverse range of industries, ensuring your cargo reaches its destination safely and on schedule.

We possess extensive experience in managing complex transportation logistics, utilizing a fleet of well-maintained vehicles and employing a team of experienced drivers. Our expertise encompasses:

  • Land transportation: We offer seamless transportation solutions by road, utilizing a network of routes and partnerships to ensure uninterrupted delivery.

  • Intermodal transportation: We combine road and rail transportation to optimize efficiency and cost-effectiveness, catering to long-distance shipments.

  • Specialized transportation: We handle the transportation of hazardous materials, oversized cargo, and temperature-sensitive goods with the utmost care and expertise.

Streamlined Transportation Solutions"


We are committed to providing our clients with reliable, cost-effective, and customer-centric transportation solutions. Our focus lies on: 

  • Safety: We prioritize the safety of your cargo and adhere to the highest safety standards, ensuring your goods reach their destination in pristine condition.

  • Timeliness: We understand the importance of timely deliveries and employ efficient route planning and real-time tracking systems to ensure your cargo arrives on schedule.

  • Cost-effectiveness: We optimize our transportation strategies to minimize costs and provide our clients with competitive rates without compromising on quality or service.
