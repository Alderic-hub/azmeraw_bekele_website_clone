Azmeraw Bekele Shines at Anuga 2023 with Ethiopian Nutritious Bounty

Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export was proud to participate in Anuga 2023 — the world's leading food and beverage trade fair held at Koelnmesse in Cologne, Germany. The biennial event, which welcomed over 137,000 trade visitors from more than 180 countries, provided an unparalleled platform for us to showcase Ethiopia's extraordinary agricultural heritage to the global food industry.

Showcasing the Best of Ethiopia

Our Anuga 2023 booth presented an curated selection of Ethiopia's finest export commodities, with a particular focus on the product lines that have earned international acclaim: specialty coffees from Yirgacheffe, Jimma, and Lekempti; premium oilseeds including sesame and niger seed; and nutritious pulses such as green mung bean and red kidney bean. Visitors were invited to experience product quality first-hand through tastings, aroma sessions, and detailed specification presentations.

The response from international buyers was extraordinary. Specialty coffee importers from Germany, the Netherlands, and Japan expressed particular interest in our Yirgacheffe Grade 2 washed coffee, drawn by its renowned floral aroma and bright citrus acidity. European food manufacturers were equally enthusiastic about our sesame and niger seed offerings, noting the exceptionally high oil content and purity levels we consistently deliver.

"Anuga 2023 was a defining moment for us — the opportunity to stand alongside the world's best food producers and demonstrate that Ethiopian commodities can meet and exceed the highest international quality standards."

Building Global Partnerships

Beyond product showcasing, Anuga served as an important relationship-building event. Over the course of four days, our team conducted more than sixty business meetings with potential buyers, agents, and distributors from across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. These conversations have already translated into new trade partnerships and letter-of-intent agreements, reinforcing our strategic goal of expanding Ethiopia's presence in premium commodity markets worldwide.

We also participated in Anuga's food innovation forums, gaining valuable insight into emerging global food trends — including the growing demand for plant-based proteins, clean-label ingredients, and sustainably sourced agricultural commodities. These trends align perfectly with Ethiopia's natural advantages, and we are actively positioning our product range to capture the opportunities they present.

Ethiopia's Agricultural Potential on the World Stage

Ethiopia's highland geography, diverse climate zones, and long agricultural tradition produce some of the world's most distinctive and nutritious food commodities. Platforms like Anuga allow us to communicate this story to buyers who are increasingly looking beyond commodity price alone, seeking origin quality, traceability, and sustainability credentials. Azmeraw Bekele is committed to being the bridge between Ethiopia's smallholder farming communities and the global buyers who value their produce.

We extend our gratitude to the Ethiopian Ministry of Trade and Regional Integration, the Ethiopian Export Promotion Agency, and our partners who supported our participation at Anuga 2023. We look forward to building on the connections made in Cologne and returning to Anuga 2025 with an even stronger showcase of Ethiopian excellence.
