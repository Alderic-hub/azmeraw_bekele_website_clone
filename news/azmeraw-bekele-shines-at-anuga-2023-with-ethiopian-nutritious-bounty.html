Azmeraw Bekele shines at Anuga 2023 with Ethiopian Nutritious Bounty!

  • Home
  • News
  • Post
  • 11 Jan

Following a triumphant appearance at the world-renowned Anuga food fair in Cologne, Germany, Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export is brimming with enthusiasm and ready to share the delectable taste of Ethiopia with the world. From October 7th to 11th, 2023, our booth buzzed with excitement as we showcased the finest pulses, grains, and other Raw materials and process materials that Ethiopia has to offer.

"Anuga was an incredible platform to connect with international buyers and showcase the immense potential of Ethiopian agriculture," enthused the Managing Director of Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export. "Our visitors were captivated by the diversity and nutritional richness of our products, and we are thrilled to begin forging new partnerships that will bring the goodness of Ethiopia to kitchens around the world."

Our spotlight at Anuga was undeniably on nutrients: lentils, chickpeas, beans, and a vibrant array of ancient grains like teff and sorghum. Packed with protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals, these humble crops are not just delicious but also contribute to a healthy and sustainable diet. Attendees were treated to samples of traditional Ethiopian dishes prepared with our finest ingredients, leaving them impressed by the unique flavors and textures.

Beyond pulses and grains, Azmeraw Bekele also presented a variety of raw materials and process materials essential for food production. From aromatic spices and herbs to natural sweeteners and oils, our offerings provided a glimpse into the rich tapestry of Ethiopian culinary ingredients.

Anuga proved to be a springboard for exciting new opportunities. We forged promising partnerships with international distributors and food manufacturers, eager to integrate the quality and nutrition of Ethiopian ingredients into their products. This is a significant step forward in our mission to promote Ethiopian agriculture on the global stage and empower local farmers.

Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export is committed to bringing the treasures of Ethiopia to the world, one nutritious bite at a time. Our Anuga success is a testament to the remarkable potential of Ethiopian agriculture and a promise of a delicious future filled with healthy and sustainable food choices.

Are you ready to discover the taste of Ethiopia? Contact us today and explore our wide range of pulses, grains, and other raw materials and process materials. Let's work together to nourish the world with the goodness of Ethiopia!
