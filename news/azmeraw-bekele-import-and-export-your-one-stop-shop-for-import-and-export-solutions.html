Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export: Your One-Stop Shop for Import and Export Solutions

In today's fast-paced global marketplace, businesses need reliable partners who can handle the complexities of international trade from end to end. Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export has built its reputation over the years as exactly that partner — a comprehensive, trusted, and results-driven enterprise that serves as a true one-stop shop for all import and export needs in and out of Ethiopia.

A Full-Spectrum Trade Partner

From sourcing premium Ethiopian agricultural commodities to facilitating the importation of industrial goods, construction materials, and commercial vehicles, Azmeraw Bekele provides an integrated suite of trade services that removes the friction typically associated with cross-border commerce. Our clients — ranging from global commodity buyers to local construction developers — consistently highlight the value of having a single, accountable partner manage their logistics, documentation, and quality assurance.

Our product portfolio spans multiple sectors: world-class specialty coffees from Yirgacheffe, Jimma, and Lekempti; oilseeds including sesame, niger, and soybean; pulses such as green mung and red kidney bean; aromatic spices like black cumin; and imported construction materials and vehicle parts including steel bars and commercial tyres. Whether you are sourcing raw materials for a food factory in Europe or procuring steel rebar for a development project in Addis Ababa, we have the expertise and network to deliver.

Integrated Logistics and Compliance

One of the biggest challenges in international trade is navigating the web of regulatory requirements, shipping documentation, phytosanitary certificates, customs procedures, and quality inspections. Azmeraw Bekele's dedicated trade operations team handles every step of this process, ensuring shipments comply with destination-country regulations and Ethiopian export standards. Our relationships with freight forwarders, port agents, and clearing houses mean that delays and complications are minimised, keeping your supply chain moving.

"We don't just move goods — we move possibilities. Every shipment we handle carries with it the trust of our clients and the quality standard of Ethiopia's finest producers."

Quality You Can Trust

Quality control is the cornerstone of everything we do. All export commodities are cleaned, graded, and tested at our processing facilities before packaging. We work with internationally accredited laboratories for moisture, purity, and contaminant testing, and we provide full certification documentation — including phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, and quality inspection reports — with every shipment. Our ISO-aligned quality management processes ensure consistency across every batch, every season.

Serving Ethiopian Agriculture and Industry

Beyond international trade, Azmeraw Bekele plays a vital role in strengthening the Ethiopian domestic economy. We support smallholder farmers by providing guaranteed off-take markets and fair pricing, while simultaneously driving industrial growth through the import of high-quality machinery, steel, and vehicle parts that power Ethiopia's construction boom. This dual role — connecting Ethiopia to the world while strengthening its foundations at home — is the mission that guides us every day.

Get in Touch

Our team is ready to discuss your specific requirements, provide competitive pricing, and design a trade solution that works for you.
