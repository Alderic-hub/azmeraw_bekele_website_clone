Red Kidney Bean (Phaseolus vulgaris)

Our red kidney beans are grown in the fertile highland zones of Ethiopia at elevations that promote slow maturation, resulting in beans with a rich, deep red colour, firm texture, and robust flavour. Machine-processed to remove damaged, discoloured, and split beans, they meet Codex Alimentarius standards and are in demand for canned food production, soups, stews, and salad bars across international markets.
