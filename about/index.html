Who We Are

A Trusted Ethiopian Enterprise Since 2011

Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export is a leading Ethiopian company with a diversified portfolio spanning import/export, transportation, manufacturing, steel bars, fuel stations, and hospitality. Established in 2011 with a paid-up capital of Birr 20,000,000.00, we have grown into a multifaceted enterprise, operating branches across Ethiopia.

Our owner, Ato Azmeraw Bekele, brings over 20 years of experience in the import and export industry. His vision and leadership have been the driving force behind our growth and success. Under his guidance, we have expanded our services, developed strategic partnerships, and built a reputation for integrity and excellence.

Our Direction

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, reliable import and export services that connect Ethiopian products to global markets, while fostering economic growth and creating employment opportunities within Ethiopia. We are committed to building lasting partnerships based on trust, transparency, and excellence.

Our Vision

To become Africa's premier multi-sector enterprise — a beacon of innovation, quality, and sustainability — that sets the standard for ethical trade and industrial excellence, while championing the economic development of Ethiopia and the broader African continent.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, and partnership are at the heart of everything we do. We believe in transparent business practices, continuous improvement, and creating value for all our stakeholders — from our clients and employees to our communities and the environment.
