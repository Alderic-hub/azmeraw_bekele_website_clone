Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to a Multi-Sector Enterprise

Founded by Ato Azmeraw Bekele with over 20 years of industry knowledge, our company has grown steadily into one of Ethiopia's most diversified trade and industrial enterprises.

2011

Company Founded

Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export was officially established with a paid-up capital of Birr 20,000,000.00. The company began operations in Addis Ababa, focusing on import and export services, leveraging the founder's 20+ years of industry experience.

2013

Export Operations Expanded

We significantly expanded our export portfolio, specializing in premium Ethiopian agricultural commodities including oilseeds, pulses, and coffee. Our reputation for quality and reliability began attracting international buyers from across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East.

2015

Transportation Division Launched

Recognizing the need for reliable freight services in Ethiopia, we launched our transportation division. This expanded our capabilities to include efficient movement of goods across the country and facilitated our own import and export logistics.

2017

Manufacturing Facility Established

We opened our state-of-the-art PP Woven bags manufacturing facility, creating over 300 jobs. The facility uses modern machinery for PP extruding, circular weaving, printing, lamination, and bag production, serving both domestic and international markets.

2019

Fuel Station & Steel Operations

We entered the energy and construction materials sectors by opening our fuel station in Jiga town and expanding our steel bar import and trading operations, further diversifying our business portfolio.

2023

Anuga International Fair Debut

Azmeraw Bekele Import & Export made a triumphant appearance at the world-renowned Anuga food fair in Cologne, Germany (October 7–11, 2023), showcasing Ethiopia's finest agricultural products and forging new international partnerships.

Today

Continued Growth & Expansion

Today, with 300+ employees across Ethiopia and operations spanning six business sectors, we continue to grow — with our hotel business development in Jiga town representing the latest chapter in our story of diversification and excellence.
